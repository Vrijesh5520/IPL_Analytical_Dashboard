# 🏏 IPL Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

The IPL Performance Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze Indian Premier League (IPL) match data and uncover meaningful insights related to team performance, venue impact, seasonal trends, and franchise success.
This dashboard transforms raw cricket data into actionable insights through data visualization, KPI tracking, and business intelligence techniques.

## Business Problem

IPL generates a large volume of match data every season. Extracting valuable insights from this data can help analysts, franchises, and cricket enthusiasts understand:
- Team performance trends
- Venue advantages
- Toss impact on match outcomes
- Franchise consistency
- Season-wise performance patterns

The objective of this project is to convert historical IPL data into an interactive analytical solution for data-driven decision-making.

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Overview Dashboard
Provides a high-level summary of IPL performance.

#### KPIs
- Total Matches
- Total Teams
- Total Venues
- Total Seasons
- Total Runs
- Total Wickets

#### Key Insights
- Tournament overview
- Match outcome distribution
- Overall performance trends
- Toss influence on results

### 2. Season Analysis
Analyzes IPL trends across different seasons.

#### Analysis Included
- Season-wise Team Performance
- Winning Trends
- Seasonal Distribution
- Toss Advantage Percentage in specific Season

#### Business Insight
Identifies dominant teams and performance changes across IPL seasons.

### 3. Venue Analysis
Evaluates how different venues influence match outcomes.

#### Analysis Included
- Matches by Venue
- Venue-wise Winning Percentage
- Toss Impact by Venue

#### Business Insight
Helps understand venue-specific advantages and playing conditions affecting match outcomes.

### 4. Franchise Analysis
Compares performance across IPL franchises.

#### Analysis Included
- Total Matches Played
- Wins & Losses
- Win Percentage
- Toss Advantage

#### Business Insight
Highlights the most successful and consistent IPL franchises based on historical performance.

## Business Insights Generated

### Team Performance
- Certain franchises consistently maintain higher win percentages across seasons.
- Historical data reveals long-term team dominance trends.

### Toss Impact
- Toss advantage varies significantly across teams and venues.
- Winning the toss does not always guarantee winning the match.

### Venue Analysis
- Some venues demonstrate strong influence on match outcomes.
- Venue conditions can impact team success rates.

### Seasonal Trends
- Competition intensity and team performance fluctuate across seasons.
- Historical trends help identify periods of franchise dominance.

### Franchise Comparison
- Strong franchises maintain performance consistency over multiple seasons.
- Comparative analysis highlights strengths and weaknesses among teams.
